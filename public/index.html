How it works

Three steps from
idea to impact.

We've removed every barrier. You don't need a degree, a company, or a grant to get started here.

01

Create your profile

Sign up in under a minute. No institution needed. Just your name, your location, and what drives you to solve problems.

02

Upload your work

Share your idea as a video demonstration, a written report, or a slide deck. All formats welcome — raw and real is fine.

03

Get discovered
